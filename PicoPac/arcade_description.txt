A tiny take on a classic.
Smaller mazes, fewer ghosts... just as much fun and danger!

Features:
3 different mazes
Pellet munching sound
Ghosts increase in speed for each level

New in Version 1.1:
New title screen
New sounds/music on game start/death/eating ghost
Pause the game by pressing A
Can view battery level on pause screen
Keeps track of high score
Can exit game from title screen
New lives at every 5k points
Past level 3 there are 3 ghosts per maze
